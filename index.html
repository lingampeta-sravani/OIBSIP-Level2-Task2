Dr.Sardar vallabhbhai Patel

Dr. Sardar Vallabhbhai patel, the People's President.

    Here's a view of Dr. Sardar Patel life:

  • 1875 - Born in Nadiad, Bombay Presidency, British India
  • 1954 - He went to London in 1910 for further studies. The 36-month course of advocacy was completed by him in just 30 months. In 1913, he came back to India and settled in Ahmedabad.
  • 1955 - moves to Madras in 1955 to study aerospace engineering in Madras Institute of Technology
  • 1917-1924 - Sardar Patel served as the first municipal commissioner of Ahmedabad
  • 1924-1928 - He served as the President
  • 1942 - Sardar patel was arrested for the third time at the time of Quit India Movement.
  • 1997 - receives Bharat Ratan.
  • 1950 - dies at the age of 83.
